ABOUT ME

I am a front-end developer, a student of Kyiv National University of Trade and Economics. I getting education related to the development and testing of software for economic purposes and not only.

The work I provide is of highest quality, fully responsive, and tested in a wide range of devices. I take great care to ensure each project is optimized and easily maintainable so you can enhance a website as your company grows.

I create responsive websites that allow the user to experience your website in the best and most appropriate way suited to the device they are using.
